Built for Precision

The BBV4 delivers consistent, linear power across the full throttle range — no spikes, no dead zones. The 2307.3 stator and N52H arc magnets produce over 1.9KG of thrust while keeping the response smooth and predictable at every input level. Two KV variants, one for controlled cinematic freestyle and one for raw aggression — same motor DNA, different character.

Durability is built in, not bolted on. The flanged titanium shaft and precision bearing stack are designed to survive repeated crashes and keep performing session after session.

  • ◈ 12N14P / N52H arc magnet — premium magnet grade for maximum efficiency and torque
  • ◈ 2307.3 stator — wider diameter for higher torque across the entire throttle range
  • ◈ Over 1.9KG thrust — serious power with ~50A peak current draw
  • ◈ AWG 20# / 150mm cables — pre-soldered and ready to mount straight out of the box
  • ◈ M5 shaft with flanged nylon lock — rock-solid prop retention, no wobble under load
  • ◈ Two 6S variants — 1960KV Mind Reader for proxy builds, 2060KV Menace for extreme freestyle
