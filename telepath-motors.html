Built to Dominate

The BlackBird BBV4 is engineered for pilots who demand the absolute maximum from their 6S build. The 2307.3 stator paired with N52H arc magnets in a 12N14P configuration delivers over 1.9KG of thrust with peak draw around 50A — raw, brutal power kept perfectly in check.

Two variants cover every use case. The Mind Reader (1960KV) is the proxy edition: smooth, efficient, and ideal for 6S long-range and cinematic work. The Menace Edition (2060KV) pushes harder for freestyle and racing — same frame, more aggression.

  • ◈ 12N14P / N52H arc magnet — premium magnet grade for maximum efficiency and torque
  • ◈ 2307.3 stator — wider diameter for higher torque across the entire throttle range
  • ◈ Over 1.9KG thrust — serious power with ~50A peak current draw
  • ◈ AWG 20# / 150mm cables — pre-soldered and ready to mount straight out of the box
  • ◈ M5 shaft with flanged nylon lock — rock-solid prop retention, no wobble under load
  • ◈ Two 6S variants — 1960KV Mind Reader for proxy builds, 2060KV Menace for extreme freestyle
