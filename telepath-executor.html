One Stack. Your Config.

The BlackBird Stack pairs an H7 dual-gyro flight controller with an AM32 F4 50A ESC — engineered together, but sold however suits your build. Take the full unit for the cleanest install, or grab just the FC or ESC if you're upgrading piece by piece.

The H7 processor with dual gyro means silky filtering headroom and the processing power to run whatever tune you throw at it. The AM32 F4 50A ESC handles anything you pair it with. More detailed specs coming soon.

  • ◈ H7 processor — high-performance CPU with headroom for demanding tunes
  • ◈ Dual gyro — redundant IMU for cleaner filtering and better in-flight stability
  • ◈ AM32 ESC firmware — open-source, actively developed, works with any protocol
  • ◈ F4 50A ESC — handles high-current builds without breaking a sweat
  • ◈ Three purchase options — full stack, FC only, or ESC only to fit any budget or upgrade path
  • ◈ More specs incoming — full pinout, dimensions and software guide coming soon
