One Frame. Two Personalities.

The BlackBird Frame is designed around two arm lengths — 4.9" and 5.1" — giving you the choice between a snappy, technical geometry and a wider, more planted feel without changing anything else about the build.

Full specs — carbon grade, weight, thickness, screw pattern and mounting options — are coming soon. The frame is being finalised and we'll update this page the moment we have numbers.

  • ◈ 4.9" arm — tighter geometry for aggressive, technical flying and snap freestyle
  • ◈ 5.1" arm — wider stance for more planted feel, better efficiency and longer range
  • ◈ Designed around the BB39 props — both arm lengths pair directly with the BlackBird prop lineup
  • ◈ Full specs coming soon — carbon type, weight, thickness and hardware details being finalised
